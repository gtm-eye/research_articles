# research_articles

This repository stores research articles we published on the Google Tag Manager system.
These works have been carried out in the context of the PhD of Gilles Mertens, defended on March 2026, in the Inria research center of the Univ. Grenoble Alpes (https://theses.fr/s352567).

- G. Mertens, N. Bielova, V. Roca, C. Santos, ``You Can't Trust Your Tag Neither: Privacy Leaks and Potential Legal Violations within the Google Tag Manager'', 10th IEEE European Symposium on Security and Privacy (EuroS&P'25), June 2025. (https://hal.science/hal-05032798), (https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.08806).

- G. Mertens, N. Bielova, V. Roca, A. Bouhoula, M. Akassab, ``Detecting and Measuring Client- and Server-Side Google Tag Manager and its Tags in 80K Websites'', 33rd ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security (CCS'26), November 2026. (camera ready not available yet).
  - This article mentions the GTM-EyE browser extension for the first time.
